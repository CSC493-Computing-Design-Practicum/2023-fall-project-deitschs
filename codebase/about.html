Sierra Christine Deitsch


My favorite quote from the show Doctor Who is, "We're all stories in the end. Just make it a good one." Filling my bookshelf with beloved stories brings me joy. I love getting lost in fantasy worlds and fawning over romances. I knew when I started working on my capstone, it had to be book related. One of my other passions is photography, which is also perfect. All of the photos on the website, except for the bookshelf images, were taken by me. When I am not reading, I am usually watching animated TV shows, playing with my silly french bulldog, or hanging out with my amazing friend group.
